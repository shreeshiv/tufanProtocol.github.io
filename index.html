MoneyBuddy

Organize your Invoices/Bills

About Us

MoneyBuddy is cloud based platform for organising Invoices/Bills. User can also track how they spend.

Features

Power by Cloud

MoneyBuddy is powered by Cloud. You can access all your Invoices/Bills from anywhere, anytime and on any device. User can access our platform using android or iOS application.

Track how you spend

Using MoneyBuddy platform, you can track how you spend your money. Download Reports and Statements according your requirement.

Multilingual Support

Support major languages. We can understand that not knowing english can't stop you to manage Invoices and Bills.

100% Safe and Secure

Your data is safe with us. We assure your privacy.

Effortless Expense Management

Using our AI enabled platform, you can plan your future financial planning and manage your expense.
